About GovAgenda

GovAgenda makes local government easy to follow. It's built for small towns — where the decisions that shape daily life often get the least coverage. The goal is simple: help every resident find what's on the agenda, follow the topics they care about, and stay informed.

GovAgenda is nonpartisan and built to serve every individual, regardless of party or position. As community interest grows, it surfaces trending topics and flags local items that connect to larger national questions, so you can see what's gaining attention and why it matters.

We pay special attention to items that touch the U.S. Constitution and the Bill of Rights — highlighting the conversations a community should be having about the rights and freedoms we all share.

Have an idea to make GovAgenda better? Head to the Community board and suggest it — no account needed.
