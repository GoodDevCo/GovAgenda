# GovAgenda — Roadmap

A public record of what's planned and what's shipped for GovAgenda — local government agendas
and meeting records, in plain view. **First city: Belle Isle, FL.**
**Live site:** https://govagenda.org/

## Planned — in priority order

_Foundation: an automated weekly refresh keeps the record current (AI-assisted for accuracy of
commissioner remarks)._

1. **Follow topics → alerts** — subscribe to a topic and get notified when it moves, by email, text (SMS), and RSS.
2. **Multi-municipality expansion** — serve other towns from the same codebase: per-city data, a simple intake for where each place publishes its agendas/minutes, per-city pages, and a directory landing page.
3. **Meetings calendar + agenda preview** — upcoming meetings and what's on them, with a subscribable calendar (add to Google/Apple Calendar).
4. **Plain-language "what this means" summaries** — a clear explainer on each item.
5. **Weekly digest email** — a Monday roundup of the topics you follow.
6. **Per-official accountability pages** — a neutral, per-commissioner record of their quotes, positions, and votes.
7. **Trending topics & search trends** — surface what the community is actually watching.
8. **National-significance / severity tag** — flag when a local item ties to a bigger national issue, tuned to your interests.
9. **Rights Watch — Bill of Rights lens** — neutrally flag topics that implicate constitutional rights, with the dialog they raise.
10. **Sticky & shareable watchlist** — pins that persist and share as a link.
11. **Shareable / embeddable topic links** — deep-link and embed any topic.
12. **Vote / position tracking** — who voted how, where the record shows it.
13. **Full-text search of source documents** — search inside agendas/minutes and jump to the page.
14. **Open data / API + downloads** — publish the dataset for others to build on.
